# Tokyo Trials Additional Sources Found

**Date:** November 21, 2025

## Additional Publicly Accessible Sources

### 1. International Criminal Court Legal Tools Database
- **URL:** https://www.fichl.org/
- **Status:** ✅ **Free online access**
- **Content:** Full transcripts of Tokyo Trial and legal discussions at UN War Crimes Commission
- **Note:** Comprehensive collection accessible without fee
- **Action:** Search for Pal's dissent and Tokyo Trial transcripts

### 2. JACAR (Japan Center for Asian Historical Records)
- **URL:** https://www.jacar.go.jp/english/
- **Status:** ✅ **Publicly accessible**
- **Content:** Digital archive of Asian historical records from National Archives of Japan, Diplomatic Archives, and National Institute for Defense Studies
- **Note:** Has searchable database
- **Action:** Search for Tokyo Trials documents and Pal references

### 3. National Archives of Japan Digital Archive
- **URL:** https://www.archives.go.jp/english/
- **Status:** ✅ **Publicly accessible**
- **Content:** Historical public records and archives
- **Note:** Can search and view images online, copies available for fee
- **Action:** Search for Tokyo Trials materials

### 4. Marquette University Law Library
- **Status:** ⚠️ **By appointment**
- **Content:** 152-volume collection of Tokyo War Crimes Trials proceedings
- **Note:** Available to researchers by appointment
- **Action:** Contact for access if needed

### 5. Shanghai Jiao Tong University Press Database
- **Status:** ✅ **Online database** (may require subscription)
- **Content:** 60 million words of Tokyo Trial records (50 million in English), 700 pictures, 50 minutes of video
- **Note:** This is the tokyotrial.cn database we already explored
- **Action:** Already explored
